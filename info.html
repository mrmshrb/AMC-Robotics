Visit ACCEL Middle College!

Accel Middle College Program is a cooperative program between East Side Union High School District and Evergreen Valley Community College. Accel juniors and seniors simultaneously attend college courses during the morning and high school classes in the afternoon. Student receive high school and college credit, which enables them to complete their high school education while experiencing college early. All classes, including high school courses, are held on the Evergreen Valley College campus.
